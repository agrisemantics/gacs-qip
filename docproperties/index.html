Scope notes [ISO 25964, p8, scope note: "note that defines or clarifies the semantic boundiaries of a concept as it is used in the structured vocabulary". Goes on to note: "A term used to label a concept can have several meanings in normal usage. A scope note is used to restrict the concept to only one of those meanings, and where necessary refers to other concepts that are included or excluded from the scope of the concept being clarified."
